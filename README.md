# Excel- Smart Store Data Analysis

## 📊 Business Questions / Key Insights

* Compare total sales vs total orders using a single visual.
* Identify the month with the highest sales and order volume.
* Analyze customer purchase behavior by gender (Men vs Women) for 2025.
* Examine different order statuses (e.g., Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded) for 2025.
* Determine the Top 10 states contributing to overall sales.
* Analyze the relationship between age group and gender based on number of orders.
* Identify the sales contribution by different channels (e.g., Online, Offline, Marketplace).
* Find the highest selling product category based on sales and quantity.

## 📊 Key Insights
* Women customers contribute significantly higher purchases compared to men, accounting for approximately 65% of total sales.
* Top performing states include Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh, contributing around 35% of overall sales.
* The adult age group (30–49 years) is the highest contributing segment, generating nearly 50% of total orders/sales.
* E-commerce channels such as Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra dominate sales, contributing approximately 80% of total revenue.

## 🎯 Final Business Recommendation
* Focus on targeting women customers aged 30–49 years in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.
* Increase engagement through personalized ads, discounts, and coupon strategies.
* Prioritize major platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra to maximize reach and sales performance.
